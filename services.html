Progressions in-home healthcare and developments in modern medicine have made treatment and therapy at home the best choice for many. People trying an alternative that balances specialist care with the familiar surroundings of the home have put their confidence in Northstar Home Healthcare at Home. Home Health Aides

our goal Our certified and guaranteed home health aides offer care, compassion, and support to help you or a family member live healthily and as freely as possible. We give just the level of care you want while respecting your privacy and your dignity.

Skilled Nursing

If you require treatment at home that needs a higher level of medical care – something that must be done by a skilled professional – we give skilled nursing assistance from highly-trained visiting nurses and nurse practitioners.

Physical Therapy

Our physical therapy specialists are ready to help you recover from an illness, injury, or surgery. If you or a loved one has experienced a stroke, traumatic brain injury, or another life-changing experience, specially-trained our physical therapists are available to assist you to get the most out of every day.

Occupational Therapy

Whether you or a family member suffer a mentally, physically, developmentally, or emotionally disabling disease, our highly-skilled occupational therapists teach you how to recover skills or compensate for a tough loss of function

Speech Therapy

Our speech-language pathologists can help increase the quality of life for you or someone you love who is encountering problems with cognitive and practical tasks, such as swallowing, reading, writing, performing computations, or who has memory, concentration impairment.
